Heritage

Four generations above the embers

What began as a rail-side curing shed is now a flagship smokehouse — still family-led, still fanatical about wood species.

Founder

Elena Voss · Master pitmaster

Elena rewired our family recipes for modern palates without surrendering the slow cadence of hardwood smoke. Her mantra: “If the chimney isn’t breathing, neither are we.”

Sourcing

Whole muscle, traceable lots

Partner ranches within a day’s drive — rotational grazing, humane handling, and marbling specs tuned for jerky, not steak vanity.

Primal intake

Eye of round and top rounds only — trimmed by hand.

Cold chain

Sub-36°F transport logs for every lot code.

Butcher craft

Consistent slice geometry for even smoke uptake.

Wood methods

Species-matched chambers

Oak for baseline sweetness, hickory for depth, mesquite for edge, pecan for dessert notes — never blended blindly.

  • Seasoned splits, never green
  • Draft-balanced stacks
  • Chamber telemetry every 90 seconds
